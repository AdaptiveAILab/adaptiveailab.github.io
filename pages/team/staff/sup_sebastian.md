## Bio

Sebastian Otte is a professor at the [Institute for Robotics and Cognitive Systems](https://www.rob.uni-luebeck.de/institut) at the [University of Lübeck](https://www.uni-luebeck.de), Germany, and head of the Adaptive AI research group. He pursued his doctoral research from 2013 to 2017 in the [Cognitive Systems](https://uni-tuebingen.de/fakultaeten/mathematisch-naturwissenschaftliche-fakultaet/fachbereiche/informatik/lehrstuehle/kognitive-systeme/) group led by [Prof. Dr. Andreas Zell](https://uni-tuebingen.de/fakultaeten/mathematisch-naturwissenschaftliche-fakultaet/fachbereiche/informatik/lehrstuehle/kognitive-systeme/the-chair/staff/prof-dr-andreas-zell/) at [Eberhard Karls University in Tübingen](), receiving his Doctor of Natural Sciences (Dr. rer. nat.) in computer science in 2017. Subsequently, he joined the [Neuro-Cognitive Modeling]() group under [Prof. Dr. Martin Butz](https://uni-tuebingen.de/fakultaeten/mathematisch-naturwissenschaftliche-fakultaet/fachbereiche/informatik/lehrstuehle/cognitive-modeling/staff/martin-butz/) also in Tübingen. In 2020, he served as a substitute professor for the professorship Distributed Intelligence at the same univesity. From 2022 to 2023 he was guest researcher in the Machine Learning group of [Prof. Dr. Sander Bohté](https://www.cwi.nl/en/people/sander-bohte/) at the [Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI)](https://www.cwi.nl) in Amsterdam, Netherlands, supported by the [Alexander von Humboldt Foundation](https://www.humboldt-foundation.de) with a [Feodor Lynen Research Fellowship](https://www.humboldt-foundation.de/vernetzen/recherche-im-humboldt-netzwerk/einzelansicht/1227664/prof-dr-sebastian-otte). In 2023, Sebastian assumed his current role as a professor for robotics at the [University of Lübeck](https://www.uni-luebeck.de).
